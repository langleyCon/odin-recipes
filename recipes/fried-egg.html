Cooking a Fried Egg for One

This recipe will show you how to coke a fried egg for one on a stovetop. There are only a few simple things you will need.

Things you'll need

  • Pan
  • Tbsp oil
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Preheat pan to medium-low heat.
  2. Once pan is hot pour tbsp of oil into pan.
  3. Once oil is heated crack open egg into pan<./li>
  4. Egg whites will begin to settle, once mostly solid flip egg gently.
  5. Wait about 30 seconds and then remove egg from pan onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy
