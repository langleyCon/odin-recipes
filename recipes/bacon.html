Cooking Bacon for One

This recipe will show you how to cook bacon for one simply in a oven. The ingredients and steps are about as simple as they get. All you need is an oven and some raw bacon.

Things you'll need

  • 4 to 6 strips of bacon
  • aluminum foil
  • baking sheet
  • an oven

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425f
  2. Line baking sheet with aluminum foil.
  3. Place bacon on baking sheet.
  4. One oven is preheated place bacon in oven for 10-15 minutes, or until fully cooked.
  5. Once bacon is fully cooked take out of oven and let rest for 2 minutes.
  6. Plate bacon and enjoy.
