Hashbrowns for One

This recipe will show you how to make hashbrowns for one. It is a super simple recipe and goes great with some eggs or bacon.

Things you'll need

  • Pan
  • Tbsp oil
  • 2/3 cups of shredded hashbrowns

Steps

  1. Preheat pan to medium-high.
  2. Add oil to pan once it is hot.
  3. Oil will begin to shimmer, add hashbrowns to pan.
  4. Spread hashbrowns evenly accross the pan
  5. Cook for about 5 - 10 minutes
  6. Once hashbrowns are crispy on the bottom flip them.
  7. Cook for an additional 2 minutes, then plate.
  8. Enjoy
